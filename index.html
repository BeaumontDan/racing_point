TECH. ON POINT.

The 2026 Formula 1 regulations include changes to the cars, power units, and safety features:

Car weight

Cars will be 30 kg lighter than the current configuration to improve agility.

Power units

The power units will use 100% sustainable fuels and have increased electric power. The internal combustion engine will produce 535 hp.

Safety

The cars will have stronger structures and undergo tougher testing. The front impact structure will deform in a crash, but a secondary frontal impact structure will protect the driver.

Overtaking

Drivers will have a new system that gives them a short burst of additional battery power when within one second of the car in front. This will replace the current drag reduction system (DRS) overtaking aid.

Engine sound

The volume of the engine sound will increase.
